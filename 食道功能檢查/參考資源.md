# 食道功能檢查 -- 參考資源

## 一、分類系統與指引 (Classification Systems & Guidelines)

### Chicago Classification v4.0

- Yadlapati R, Kahrilas PJ, Fox MR, et al. Esophageal motility disorders on high-resolution manometry: Chicago Classification version 4.0. *Neurogastroenterology & Motility*. 2021;33(1):e14058.
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1111/nmo.14058
  - 52 位國際專家、15 國、469 位健康志願者資料
  - 首次納入仰臥位 + 直立位 + 激發測試
  - 建立三套商用 HRM 系統的分別正常值

### Lyon Consensus 2.0

- Gyawali CP, Yadlapati R, Fass R, et al. Updates to the modern diagnosis of GERD: Lyon Consensus 2.0. *Gut*. 2024;73(2):361-371.
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1136/gutjnl-2023-330616
  - AET > 6% 為確定性 GERD 證據
  - AET < 4% 且逆流 < 40 次排除 GERD
  - 引入輔助指標：BI、PSPWI、組織病理學

### Lyon Consensus 1.0（原始版）

- Gyawali CP, Kahrilas PJ, Savarino E, et al. Modern diagnosis of GERD: the Lyon Consensus. *Gut*. 2018;67(7):1351-1362.
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1136/gutjnl-2017-314722

### Chicago Classification 歷史版本

- Kahrilas PJ, Bredenoord AJ, Fox M, et al. The Chicago Classification of esophageal motility disorders, v3.0. *Neurogastroenterology & Motility*. 2015;27(2):160-174.
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1111/nmo.12477

## 二、重要相關文獻 (Key Publications)

### HRM 技術與應用

- Pandolfino JE, Ghosh SK, Rice J, et al. Classifying esophageal motility by pressure topography characteristics: a study of 400 patients and 75 controls. *American Journal of Gastroenterology*. 2008;103(1):27-37.
  - HRM 等壓圖分類的奠基研究

- Fox MR, Bredenoord AJ. Oesophageal high-resolution manometry: moving from research into clinical practice. *Gut*. 2008;57(3):405-423.
  - HRM 從研究到臨床應用的重要回顧

### FLIP 技術

- Hirano I, Pandolfino JE, Boeckxstaens GE. Functional Lumen Imaging Probe for the Management of Esophageal Disorders: Expert Review from the Clinical Practice Updates Committee of the AGA Institute. *Clinical Gastroenterology and Hepatology*. 2017;15(3):325-334.
  - AGA 專家回顧 FLIP 在食道疾病中的應用

- Carlson DA, Kahrilas PJ, Lin Z, et al. Evaluation of Esophageal Motility Utilizing the Functional Lumen Imaging Probe. *American Journal of Gastroenterology*. 2016;111(12):1726-1735.
  - FLIP panometry 的概念與應用

### AI 輔助 HRM 判讀

- Systematic review on AI-assisted HRM interpretation (2025). *JMIR*. 17 項研究，4,588 名患者，6 個國家。
  - 評估 AI 在 HRM 判讀中的應用：解剖標誌辨識、弛緩不能症分類、Chicago Classification 自動化
  - 82% 的研究發表於 2020 年之後

- Farah J, et al. Artificial intelligence in high-resolution manometry: a systematic review. *Neurogastroenterology & Motility*. 2025.
  - CNN 與 ML 技術在 Clouse Plot 影像分析與 HRM 數值參數分類中的應用
  - 專家間判讀差異達 30-40%，AI 有潛力提高診斷一致性

### 逆流監測

- Roman S, Gyawali CP, Savarino E, et al. Ambulatory Reflux Monitoring for Diagnosis of Gastro-Esophageal Reflux Disease: Update of the Porto Consensus and Recommendations from an International Consensus Group. *Neurogastroenterology & Motility*. 2017;29(10):1-15.
  - 逆流監測的國際共識

### 食道弛緩不能症

- Vaezi MF, Pandolfino JE, Yadlapati RH, et al. ACG Clinical Guidelines: Diagnosis and Management of Achalasia. *American Journal of Gastroenterology*. 2020;115(9):1393-1411.
  - ACG 食道弛緩不能症臨床指引
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.14309/ajg.0000000000000731

### GERD 整體管理

- Katz PO, Dunbar KB, Schnoll-Sussman FH, et al. ACG Clinical Guideline for the Diagnosis and Management of Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease. *American Journal of Gastroenterology*. 2022;117(1):27-56.
  - ACG GERD 臨床指引最新版
  - DOI: https://doi.org/10.14309/ajg.0000000000001538
