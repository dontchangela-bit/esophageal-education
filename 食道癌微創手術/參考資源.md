# 參考資源

本文件彙整食道癌微創手術相關的國際指引、關鍵臨床試驗、醫院資源及病患教育資源，供臨床工作者及患者參考。

## 一、國際指引 (International Guidelines)

### NCCN (National Comprehensive Cancer Network)

- **NCCN Clinical Practice Guidelines in Oncology: Esophageal and Esophagogastric Junction Cancers (Version 1.2025)**
  - 網址：https://www.nccn.org/professionals/physician_gls/pdf/esophageal.pdf
  - 內容：完整的食道癌及胃食道交界癌治療指引，涵蓋診斷、分期、手術、化療、放療、免疫治療及追蹤
  - 更新頻率：每年多次更新

### ESMO (European Society for Medical Oncology)

- **ESMO Clinical Practice Guideline: Oesophageal Cancer (2022)**
  - 文獻：Lordick F, et al. Oesophageal cancer: ESMO Clinical Practice Guideline. Ann Oncol. 2022;33(10):992-1004.
  - 網址：https://www.esmo.org/guidelines/gastrointestinal-cancers/oesophageal-cancer
  - 內容：歐洲食道癌臨床實務指引，強調 MDT、分期檢查標準化、治療決策

- **ESMO 2025 Interim Update**
  - 內容：更新免疫治療 (ICI) 在晚期食道鱗狀細胞癌 (SCC) 的一線治療建議

### JSMO Pan-Asian Adapted ESMO Guidelines

- **Pan-Asian adapted ESMO Clinical Practice Guidelines for the management of patients with oesophageal cancer**
  - 內容：基於 ESMO 指引的亞太地區調整版，考量亞洲 SCC 為主的流行病學特徵
  - 適用：台灣、日本、韓國、中國等亞太地區

### 其他相關指引

- **STS/ESTS Guidelines for Credentialing and Maintaining Privileges for Esophagectomy**
  - 手術量與品質標準建議

- **ERAS Society Guidelines: Enhanced Recovery After Esophagectomy**
  - 網址：https://www.erassociety.org/
  - 內容：食道切除術加速康復外科協定

## 二、關鍵臨床試驗 (Key Clinical Trials)

### MIRO Trial

- **全名：** Minimally Invasive Oesophagectomy vs Open Oesophagectomy
- **設計：** 多中心隨機對照試驗 (RCT)，法國
- **比較：** Hybrid MIE (腹腔鏡 + 開胸) vs. Open esophagectomy
- **關鍵結果：** MIE 組 30 天重大併發症率 35.9% vs. Open 64.4% (p < 0.001)；3 年 OS 58.4%，MIE 不劣於 Open
- **文獻：** Mariette C, et al. Hybrid Minimally Invasive Esophagectomy for Esophageal Cancer. N Engl J Med. 2019;380(2):152-162.
- **DOI：** 10.1056/NEJMoa1805101

### ROBOT Trial

- **全名：** Robot-Assisted Minimally Invasive Thoracolaparoscopic Esophagectomy vs Open Transthoracic Esophagectomy
- **設計：** 單中心 RCT，荷蘭 (UMC Utrecht)
- **比較：** RAMIE vs. Open esophagectomy
- **關鍵結果：** RAMIE 組總併發症率 59% vs. Open 80% (p = 0.02)；肺部併發症 32% vs. 58% (p = 0.005)
- **文獻：** van der Sluis PC, et al. Robot-assisted minimally invasive thoracolaparoscopic esophagectomy versus open transthoracic esophagectomy for resectable esophageal cancer: a randomized controlled trial. Ann Surg. 2019;269(4):621-630.
- **DOI：** 10.1097/SLA.0000000000003031

### TIME Trial

- **全名：** Traditional Invasive vs Minimally Invasive Esophagectomy
- **設計：** 多中心 RCT，荷蘭
- **比較：** Total MIE vs. Open esophagectomy
- **關鍵結果：** MIE 組肺部感染率 12% vs. Open 34% (p = 0.005)；術後 6 週 QoL 顯著較佳
- **文獻：** Biere SS, et al. Minimally invasive versus open oesophagectomy for patients with oesophageal cancer: a multicentre, open-label, randomised controlled trial. Lancet. 2012;379(9829):1887-1892.
- **DOI：** 10.1016/S0140-6736(12)60516-9

### CROSS Trial

- **全名：** Chemoradiotherapy for Oesophageal Cancer Followed by Surgery Study
- **設計：** 多中心 RCT，荷蘭
- **比較：** 術前化放療 (carboplatin/paclitaxel + 41.4 Gy) + 手術 vs. 單純手術
- **關鍵結果：** nCRT + 手術組中位 OS 49.4 月 vs. 手術組 24.0 月 (p = 0.003)；pCR 率 SCC 49%, EAC 23%
- **文獻：** van Hagen P, et al. Preoperative chemoradiotherapy for esophageal or junctional cancer. N Engl J Med. 2012;366(22):2074-2084.
- **DOI：** 10.1056/NEJMoa1112088

### CheckMate 577

- **全名：** Adjuvant Nivolumab in Resected Esophageal or Gastroesophageal Junction Cancer
- **設計：** 全球多中心 Phase III RCT
- **比較：** 術後 Nivolumab 輔助治療 vs. 安慰劑（nCRT 後手術且 non-pCR 患者）
- **關鍵結果：** Nivolumab 組中位 DFS 22.4 月 vs. 安慰劑 11.0 月 (HR 0.69, p < 0.001)
- **文獻：** Kelly RJ, et al. Adjuvant Nivolumab in Resected Esophageal or Gastroesophageal Junction Cancer. N Engl J Med. 2021;384(13):1191-1203.
- **DOI：** 10.1056/NEJMoa2032125
